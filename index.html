✨ Meet the Unicorns ✨
🎮 How to Play 🎮
🦄 Meet Wave!
Wave is a magical unicorn who wants to paint the whole world with rainbow colors! Help her spread magic and joy everywhere.
👆 Tap to Move
Tap anywhere on the screen to move Wave. She'll run toward where you tap! Try tapping left and right to see her turn.
✨ Double-Tap for Magic
Double-tap (tap twice quickly) to shoot magical beams! The beams will pop all the colorful balloons and paint the world with magic.
🎈 Pop the Balloons!
Pop as many balloons as you can to paint the world with rainbows. Each balloon you pop adds more color to the landscape!
⚡ Magic Meter
Watch the magic meter under Wave. When it fills up (lightning bolt shows), you can shoot magic beams! It fills back up quickly.
🌈 Paint the World
Keep playing and watch the world turn from gray to beautiful rainbow colors! When everything is painted, you've made the whole world magical!
✨ Have Fun!
There's no winning or losing - just have fun spreading magic and color! Wave never gives up, and neither should you!
